About

Suah Kim spent his time living in many different countries. He finished undergraduate studies in combinatorics and optimization (Honours in Bachelor of Mathematics)  in  University of Waterloo in Canada. He then continued graduate studies in Information Security at Korea University in South Korea.  

Currently, he is working as a postdoctral researcher at Korea University.
