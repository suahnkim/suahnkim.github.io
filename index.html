About

Suah Kim lived in  Korea, Japan, United States, Japan, and China. He finished undergraduate studies in Combinatorics and Optimization (Honours in Bachelor of Mathematics)  from  University of Waterloo in Canada. He then continued graduate studies in information security at Korea University in South Korea.  

Currently he is working as a postdoctral researcher at Korea University researching topics around signal processing and security.
